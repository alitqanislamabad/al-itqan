Code of Conduct for Students

There are clear codes of conduct established for students in this institution. Every student must prioritize academic and moral values.

Complete avoidance of fights and mutual disputes is required.

One must completely stay away from political and party activities.

Every student's attention should be focused solely on education and training.

Time Schedule

The daily time and activity schedule for students is as follows:

7:00 AM to 11:00 AM - Class time

11:00 AM to 1:00 PM - Nap and rest

1:30 PM - Zuhr prayer followed by half an hour lunch break

Until Asr - Time for memorizing adhkar

Asr to Maghrib - Break and walking

Maghrib to Isha - Study and lesson preparation

After Isha prayer - Dinner then revision and study until 11:00 PM

Special Rules for Ifta Specialization

The following rules are mandatory for ifta students:

Every fatwa must be corrected by at least three teachers (wherein the institution's pattern, interpretation, and correctness of the answer according to the question is checked).

After writing "Al-Jawab Al-Sahih" from two teachers, the Darul Ifta seal is affixed.

Keep the original fatwa with yourself while submitting a copy (print) for university records.

All fatwas must be composed on computer; handwritten fatwas are not acceptable.

It is necessary to submit a word file copy of every fatwa in the institution's records.

Every fatwa must follow the specified pattern.

Laptop usage is mandatory for ifta students to ensure work is done with quality and order.

Leave and Attendance Policy

The institution provides holidays at three times during the academic year: one at Eid-ul-Adha, similarly at the end of quarterly exams, and then after the semi-annual exams. The purpose of these holidays is to give teachers and students a suitable break from teaching activities and an opportunity to prepare for the next stage. The duration of holidays is determined by mutual consultation of teachers and the needs of the institution.

Besides these occasions, regularity and attendance are mandatory for students. No student is allowed leave without permission. If any compulsion arises, prior information is necessary. The institution reserves the right to take disciplinary action for repeated absences or violation of rules.
